Regular IROS paper teasers

One of the fundamental reasons for organizing this workshop is the lack of room for scientists interested in deformable objects to meet. We have no dedicated publications, no dedicated sessions in conferences, not even a RAS keyword to rally under!
If you present works related to robotic deformable object manipulation in the main body of IROS, hit us up with the ID and time of your presentation, we'll try our best to advertise this information. Please use the contact email specified on this website to do so. If we have the time, we could even share a slide presenting your works during the workshop.
